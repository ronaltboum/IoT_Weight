Calibrate Your Scale

You'll need to perform a calibration for your scale so it will give an accurate result.
to calibrate calibrate the weight-scale you can either manually enter the SCALE and OFFSET values of it, or just follow these instuctions to calculate these values:
  1. Take an object with a known weight
  2. Write its weight in the textbox below
  3. Make sure there is nothing on the weight-scale (i.e. you 'weighing air')
  4. Click on 'Calibrate'
  5. Place your object on the weight-scale
  6. Click on 'Calibrate' again
  7. The server will calculate the values of SCALE and OFFSET that are suitable for your device.
