Calibrate Your Scale

You'll need to perform a calibration for your scale so it will give an accurate result.
calibration is simple, just follow these instuctions:
  1. Make sure there is nothing on the scale
  2. Set the scale's offset by simply pressing on 'Set Offset' (this operation might take a few seconds)
  3. Now, place on the scale an object which its weight is known to you
  4. Click on 'Set Scale' (this operation might take a few seconds)
  5. Write the object's weight in the textbox below
  6. click on 'Calibrate'
  7. All Set! The device will calculate the values of SCALE and OFFSET that are suitable for you.
